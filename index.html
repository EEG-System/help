Goal

This is the home page for the project documentation. This page takes you to the pieces neccesary that makes the project possible.

We are involved in an ongoing project of the Electroencephalography system with collaboration of The University of Texas at El Paso and Sands Research. Our goal is to gather EEG signals to be able to predict seizures in people that suffer from epilepsy.
