This HELP ME page has the goal to facilitate the documentation and the usage of the files provided.

Goal

The purpose of this page it is to exlpain why and where the ADS1299 is use within the EEG System.
We are involved in an ongoing project of the Electroencephalography system. Our goal is to gather EEG signals to be able to predict seizures in people that suffer from epilepsy.

Pre-requirements

  1. ADS1299 Datasheet
  2. ADS1299EEG-FE User Guide
  3. MMBO Datasheet

Details

To be able to gather EEG signal, the need of a high and low understading of a Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC) chip, ADS1299, is key.
