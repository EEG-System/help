Goal

The purpose of this page is to exlpain why and where the ADS1299 is used within the EEG System.
We are involved in an ongoing project of the Electroencephalography system. Our goal is to gather EEG signals to be able to predict seizures in people that suffer from epilepsy.

Pre-requirements

  1. ADS1299 Datasheet
  2. ADS1299EEG-FE User Guide
  3. MMBO Datasheet

Details

To be able to gather EEG signal, the need of a high and low understading of a Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC) chip, ADS1299, is key.

  • SPI Communication.
  • ADS1299 Power Supply.
  • Configurations
  • ADS1299 Capabilities.

Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI)

  • SCLK: Serial Clock (output from master)
  • MOSI: Master Out Slave In (data output from master)
  • MISO: Master In Slave Out (data output from slave)
  • SS: Slave Select (often active low, output from master)
